About me

I started my musical journey when still a little boy. After high-school I decided to go out and explore the world. I spent a couple of years moving around Buenos Aires and all of a sudden I found myself playing in Europe. Along the way I learnt how to play various instruments which helped me to open my mind towards arranging. After having worked in the entertainment business in Europe, life took me to Australia to deepen my knowledge and go back to my first love: The Guitar.
